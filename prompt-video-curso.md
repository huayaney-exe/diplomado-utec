# Prompt para Generador de Video AI

## Herramientas recomendadas
- **Runway Gen-3** (runway.ml)
- **Pika Labs** (pika.art)
- **Kling AI** (klingai.com)
- **Luma Dream Machine** (lumalabs.ai)

---

## Prompt Principal (Ingles - mejor rendimiento)

```
A professional business setting in a modern university classroom. A confident instructor in smart casual attire stands next to a large digital whiteboard displaying product management diagrams and flowcharts. The instructor gestures naturally while explaining concepts. Soft natural lighting from large windows. Clean, minimalist corporate aesthetic. Slight camera movement, cinematic depth of field. Professional educational atmosphere. 4K quality.
```

---

## Prompt Alternativo 1: Enfoque en tecnologia

```
Close-up of hands working on a laptop screen showing wireframes and product prototypes. Transition to a wider shot revealing a collaborative workspace with multiple professionals discussing around a screen. Modern tech office environment with glass walls and plants. Warm ambient lighting. Smooth camera pan. Professional corporate video style.
```

---

## Prompt Alternativo 2: Enfoque en colaboracion

```
A diverse team of professionals gathered around a conference table reviewing product documentation. Digital screens in the background show analytics dashboards and user flow diagrams. One person presents while others take notes on laptops. Modern corporate environment with UTEC university branding colors (cyan/turquoise accents). Natural movement, professional lighting.
```

## Prompt Alternativo 3: Proceso de producto

```
Split screen montage: Left side shows a person sketching wireframes on paper, transitioning to the same design appearing on a computer screen. Right side shows user testing on a mobile device with a heatmap overlay visualization. Modern workspace setting. Clean transitions. Professional educational content style. Cyan and black color accents.
```

## Parametros recomendados

| Parametro | Valor |
|-----------|-------|
| Duracion | 5-10 segundos |
| Aspect Ratio | 16:9 |
| Estilo | Cinematico / Corporativo |
| Movimiento de camara | Suave, minimo |
| Calidad | Alta (4K si disponible) |

---

## Imagen de referencia sugerida

Usa como imagen de referencia inicial:
- Foto de un aula moderna de universidad
- Persona presentando frente a pantalla digital
- Ambiente corporativo profesional

---

## Notas de uso

1. **Runway Gen-3**: Mejor para movimientos naturales y calidad cinematica
2. **Pika Labs**: Bueno para animaciones sutiles desde imagen estatica
3. **Kling AI**: Excelente para duracion extendida (hasta 10s)
4. **Luma**: Mejor coherencia de escena y transiciones

Para mejores resultados, genera 3-4 variaciones y selecciona la mejor.
